# SPP AI BRAIN

Version: 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

The AI Brain is the core intelligence of SPP.

It does not display data.

It interprets data, ranks decisions, detects risks, finds opportunities, and guides the owner.

---

# MAIN BRAIN MODULES

Executive Brain

Unified Brain

Decision Engine

Opportunity Engine

Contract Brain

Financial Brain

Maintenance Brain

Risk Brain

Property Memory

Owner Learning Engine

---

# BRAIN RULES

The Brain must always answer:

What should the owner do now?

Why does it matter?

What is the expected outcome?

---

# NEVER DO THIS

Never show raw debug values.

Never show negative dates.

Never use technical language.

Never make the owner analyze data manually.

Never create recommendations without a reason.

---

# ALWAYS DO THIS

Recommend the next action.

Explain the impact.

Rank priorities.

Separate urgent from non-urgent.

Detect opportunities, not only risks.

Use professional property-manager language.

---

# FINAL PRINCIPLE

SPP succeeds when the owner opens the app and immediately knows what to do next.
